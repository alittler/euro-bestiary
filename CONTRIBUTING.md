# Contribution Guide

We welcome additions and corrections to this dataset!

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork** the repository.
2. **Add or update creatures** in `data/creatures.csv` (use spreadsheet or CSV editor).
3. **Follow the field descriptions** in `data-dictionary.md`.
4. **Submit a Pull Request** explaining your change.

## Guidelines

- Provide sources or references where possible.
- Avoid duplication—check if your entry exists.
- Keep entries concise and factual.
- Use UTF-8 encoding throughout.

Thank you for helping expand this open folklore resource!
